What is The 20-0 Game?

The 20-0 Game is a free daily NFL draft game you play in your browser. Each pick puts a random NFL team on the clock — draft their best quarterback, running back, receivers, offensive line, and a defensive captain from over 14,000 real player seasons (1999–2024, plus hand-rated legends like Jerry Rice, Barry Sanders, Lawrence Taylor, and Deion Sanders). Your defensive captain's position changes how your defense plays: a cornerback shuts down passing attacks, a defensive lineman stuffs the run, a linebacker defends both.

When your roster is set, simulate a full 17-game season and the playoffs. Win every game to finish 20-0 — perfect and immortal. A new daily challenge drops every day with the same teams for everyone, so you can share your score and compete with friends on the daily leaderboard.
