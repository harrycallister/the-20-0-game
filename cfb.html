What is The 15-0 Game?

The 15-0 Game is a free daily college football draft game you play in your browser. Each pick puts a random program-season on the clock — Alabama 2020, USC 2004, Miami 2001, Nebraska 1995 — and you draft their best quarterback, running back, receivers, offensive line, and a defensive captain from thousands of real player seasons (2001–2024, plus hand-rated legends like Herschel Walker, Barry Sanders, Bo Jackson, and Charles Woodson). Your defensive captain's position changes how your defense plays: a cornerback shuts down passing attacks, a defensive lineman stuffs the run, a linebacker defends both.

When your roster is set, simulate a full 12-game season and the College Football Playoff. Win every game — the regular season, the quarterfinal, the semifinal, and the national championship — to finish 15-0, perfect and immortal. A new daily challenge drops every day with the same teams for everyone, so you can share your score and compete with friends on the daily leaderboard.
